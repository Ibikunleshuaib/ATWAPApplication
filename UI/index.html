About Us

ATWAP Oyo State chapter is an association established to protect and promote the interest of her members across the state. We are the recognised body of table and sachet water producers in Oyo State. Our mission is to foster the interest of our memebers and as well provide pure table and sachet water for people's consumption

Grow your business

We believe collaboration amongst members is beneficial to all. Connect with experienced professionals and businessmen in the table and sachet water industry and be properly guided. Network with members and get your business the assistance it needs to grow.

Be Inspired

Business ideas and actualization become easier in an environment where owners of similar businesses collaborate and share ideas. ATWAP provides you the unique oppotunity to be inspired and be creative with your business. Get first hand information in the industry necessary to make smart business decisions.

Become a member today

By joining ATWAP, you will have access to all priviledges available to members ranging from protection of the interest of your business to training and workshops that ignite your business mindset to networking and collaborating with professionals in the industry.

We provide pure drinking water for the society

ATWAP Oyo State chapter ensures pure water producers across the state provide clean table and sachet water that is free of germs and healthy for consumption. Quality cannot be compromised.

We believe we have a significant role to play in ensuring that everyone across the state has access to pure table and sachet water. This we take very seriously and mechanisms have been put in place to ensure our members' strict compliance to standard operating procedures as required by NAFDAC.
